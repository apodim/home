About the Game

Greedy Drivers is the original racing game that started it all. This is where you race against the clock to collect as many coins as possible in thrilling speed challenges. It's the classic that inspired the entire Greedy Drivers series.

Gameplay

Race through challenging tracks, gather coins, and compete for the fastest times. Perfect for players who love classic arcade-style racing games with a coin-collecting twist.
