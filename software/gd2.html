Greedy Drivers 2

Get ready to rev your engines and explore like never before!

About the Game

Greedy Drivers 2 is a racing game that takes the high-octane action of the original and cranks it up to the next level. Dive into a thrilling mix of competitive racing and open-world exploration, all set against the stunning backdrop of Ioannina, Greece.

Key Features

🚗 Racing Mode

Challenge yourself and other drivers in adrenaline-fueled races. Speed through city streets, countryside roads, and iconic landmarks to prove you're the fastest driver in Ioannina.

🌍 Explore Mode

Prefer to take it slow? Explore the meticulously crafted open-world map of Ioannina at your own pace. Discover hidden shortcuts, breathtaking views, and local landmarks.

📍 Authentic Map Design

Experience a map inspired by the beauty and charm of Ioannina, with its picturesque streets, historic sites, and stunning natural surroundings.

🎮 Dynamic Gameplay

Whether you're chasing victory in competitive races or roaming free to uncover every corner of the map, enjoy a blend of intense and laid-back experiences.

🗺️ Multiple DLC Expansions

Extend your adventure with the Nafpaktos DLC and other upcoming expansions bringing new locations and challenges.
