Bio

Apodim is the owner and primary developer of Apodim's Software, a content and software creation venture established in 2024. With a passion for innovative software development and game design, Apodim has created and published over 10 applications and games. Most of the software is built independently by Apodim, utilizing modern development tools like GDevelop (a no-code game engine) and MIT App Inventor.
